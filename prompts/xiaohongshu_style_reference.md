# Xiaohongshu Style Reference

Evidence status: includes one operator-viewed Xiaohongshu sample and broader
public-source style guidance. Use this for structure, hook style, pacing, and
cover direction, not as proof of platform performance. Do not treat it as
verified admissions evidence.

Use these patterns for Xiaohongshu postgraduate-to-PhD content:

- Start from a real applicant anxiety or mistake.
- Prefer conflict/correction hooks: `不是先套磁`, `第一步做反了`, `先别急着群发邮件`.
- Sound like a warm senior schoolmate: friendly, specific, lightly cute, not
  like an official article.
- Use short mobile lines and practical steps.
- Make one clear judgment, then explain the reason and action path.
- Add a clear Xiaohongshu expression layer: feminine, friendly, slightly cute,
  with useful advice underneath.
- Natural address words are allowed in moderation: `姐妹`, `宝子`, `uu`, `学妹`.
- Conversational particles should appear naturally: `哦`, `哟`, `呀`, `啊`, `嘛`,
  `呢`, `哈`. Put them in hooks, transitions, and soft reminders.
- For list-heavy education posts, use emoji as section navigation and `结构标识`,
  not random decoration. A strong pattern is: `👉💧` opening hook, `👇`
  classification lead-in, `📍` route sections, `🔥` benefits/conditions, `✅`
  benefit bullets, `🎓` major/program pool, `😎` direct FAQ confidence line, and
  `💓` application-condition or soft CTA close.
- Unless the task says not to, include 5-9 structural emojis across route/list
  posts. Add sticker codes or light kaomoji only as a secondary layer, such as
  `[笑哭R]`, `[哭惹R]`, `[哇R]`, `[赞R]`, `[doge]`, `[蹲后续H]`.
- Use expressive punctuation for rhythm: `～`, `！！`, `？`, `……`. Do not stack
  more than two strong marks in a row.
- Add at least one short parenthetical aside for 表情包感, for example:
  `（先别慌）`, `（真的会被看出来）`, `（这一步很亏）`.
- Avoid guaranteed outcomes, fear-based urgency, unsupported admissions claims,
  fake official endorsements, and fake scarcity.
- Keep all facts traceable to provided knowledge context and collected trend
  assets.

Copy structure:

1. Reader question or anxiety.
2. Counter-intuitive but safe answer.
3. Why the common behavior fails.
4. Route taxonomy, checklist, or decision table.
5. Reassuring close with human-review-safe caveat.

Example opening patterns:

- `姐妹们，硕升博真的不是先套磁呀！！[哭惹R]`
- `这个顺序很多人一开始就做反了……`
- `先别急着群发邮件哦，你得先让导师看懂你能研究什么。`
- `宝子，如果方向还没拆清楚，邮件写得再礼貌也容易像模板（真的）。`

Keyword title angles:

- `水博`: myth-busting, route sorting, risk clarification, identity defense, and
  decision filters. Useful shapes include `想读水博前，先把这几条路看明白呀`,
  `不是所有海外博士都适合你`, and `水博有用吗？先看用途和认证`.
- `硕升博`: sequence correction, route comparison, supervisor matching, and
  warm senior-schoolmate reminders. Useful shapes include `硕升博第一步，不是先套磁`,
  `硕升博怎么选？先把这 3 条路分清楚`, and `宝子，硕升博别急着群发邮件呀`.
- `土博`: employment anxiety, domestic/overseas contrast, and self-positioning.
  Useful shapes include `土博毕业后还值不值得？先看就业这件事`, `土博和海博，不是只看名字`,
  and `土博不是原罪，但履历要这样讲清楚`.
- `升博`: broad route planning, common first-step mistakes, material checklists,
  and gentle direction-finding. Useful shapes include `升博前先别急，路线要先分清`,
  `很多人升博第一步就做反了`, and `想升博但没方向？先从这张表开始哦`.

Operator-viewed case: `水博榜`

- Source shape: `请大数据把这个水博榜推给想读在职博士的你`.
- Cover structure: route matrix / school pool board, not another generic desk
  cover.
- Buckets observed: 国内、国际、港澳、合办、海外.
- Each item pairs a school/project name with one tiny decision cue such as
  `周末读`, `线上+线下`, `中文读`, `寒暑假`, `免答辩`, or approximate price.
- Reuse the structure, not the unverified claims. If the knowledge base does not
  contain verified school data, use category labels and decision criteria
  instead of inventing school names.

Cover/image structure:

- Use the exact approved content title as the primary cover headline.
- Vary cover visuals. Do not always use the same desk, sticky notes, scrapbook
  paper, coral/mint checklist, and red underline formula.
- For route/list posts, consider a `路线/榜单型封面`: 4-5 route buckets, verified
  school/project pool, and one cue per item.
- Other acceptable visual routes: phone screenshot collage, academic blueprint,
  editorial magazine page, workflow board, library research mood, blackboard
  annotation, minimal typography poster, or soft cute notebook.
- Use check/cross marks, circled keywords, numbered checklists, short chips, or
  before/after contrast only when they fit the chosen route.
- Avoid fake university seals, fake admission letters, guaranteed-offer badges,
  copied platform UI, or official Xiaohongshu/Douyin logos.

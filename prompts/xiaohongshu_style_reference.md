# Xiaohongshu Style Reference — 引流变现版

Evidence status: includes operator-viewed Xiaohongshu samples and public-source
style guidance. Use this for structure, hook style, pacing, CTA design, and
cover direction.

## Persona Design

The creator persona is a warm, credible "senior schoolmate" who has personal
experience with the postgraduate-to-PhD journey. She is:

- A past applicant who succeeded despite disadvantages (e.g., 双非, 无论文,
  在职). This creates "I've been there" trust.
- Someone who has helped others through the process. This establishes
  authority without bragging.
- Friendly and approachable, but specific and practical. No empty motivation.

Persona reinforcement should appear naturally in 1-2 lines, not as a separate
bio section. Examples:
- "我自己就是从双非无论文硬申上岸的，这些坑我都踩过。"
- "辅导过几十位申博考生后发现，第一步做对的都有共同点。"

## Funnel Topic Matrix

Content should map to the reader's journey. Each post serves a funnel stage:

| Stage | Topic Type | Purpose | Example Topic | CTA Tier |
|-------|-----------|---------|---------------|----------|
| Awareness | Pain point | Stop scroll, trigger anxiety | "发了30封套磁信0回复？" | A |
| Awareness | Mistake/避坑 | Loss aversion | "90%申博人栽在这8个套磁坑" | A |
| Interest | Route/路线 | Save-worthy comparison | "硕升博3条路线怎么选" | C |
| Interest | Timeline | Practical reference | "申博全流程时间线" | C |
| Desire | Case study | Trust building | "双非无论文如何逆袭上岸" | B |
| Desire | Value/含金量 | Objection handling | "在职博士含金量怎么回答" | B |
| Action | Consultation | Direct conversion | "怎么判断自己适不适合申博" | A |

Pain point and mistake topics should make up 60%+ of content for maximum
engagement and conversion. Route and timeline topics are for SEO and saves.

## Copy Structure (7-Layer Funnel)

1. Pain-point hook (2-3 lines with searchable keywords)
2. Empathy bridge (1-2 lines acknowledging feelings)
3. Core value (practical body with route/step/comparison format)
4. Mistake highlight (1 short paragraph triggering loss aversion)
5. Persona reinforcement (1-2 lines of natural self-reference)
6. Soft CTA (compliant call-to-action, varying tiers)
7. Compliance-safe closing (verification reminder if needed)

## Hook Patterns

Use these proven opening patterns:

- Counter-intuitive: "姐妹们，硕升博真的不是先套磁呀！！[哭惹R]"
- Pain resonance: "套磁发了几十封，全部石沉大海……是不是你？"
- Data shock: "90%的申博人第一步就做反了，看看你中招没"
- Identity: "作为刚上岸的在职博士，踩过的坑全整理出来了"

The first 100 characters must contain at least one searchable keyword:
申博, 套磁, 博士申请, 在职博士, 研究计划书, 博导, 硕升博.

## CTA Templates (Compliant)

**Tier A — Engagement (drives comments, highest conversion):**
"你目前申博卡在哪一步？
A.不知道适不适合读博
B.套磁信没回复
C.研究计划书不会写
D.找不到适配导师
评论区留下选项，我挑3位免费做申博规划诊断🎁"

**Tier B — Resource (drives saves and DMs):**
"整理了一份《申博避坑清单》，踩坑后总结的，
有需要的私信交流～"

**Tier C — Follow (drives follows, safest):**
"觉得有用记得⭐收藏，申博路上不迷路～
关注我，持续分享申博干货和避坑经验。"

Rotate CTA tiers. Do not repeat the same tier in consecutive posts.

## Emoji System

Structural emojis for scanning (5-9 per post):
- `👉💧` opening hook
- `👇` classification lead-in
- `📍` route/list sections
- `🔥` advantages or conditions
- `✅` benefit bullets
- `🎓` major/program pool
- `😎` FAQ confidence line
- `💓` CTA close

Sticker codes as secondary layer: `[笑哭R]`, `[哭惹R]`, `[哇R]`,
`[赞R]`, `[doge]`, `[蹲后续H]`.

## Conversational Layer

- Particles: `哦`, `哟`, `呀`, `啊`, `嘛`, `呢`, `哈` — in hooks and
  transitions, not every sentence.
- Parenthetical asides: `（先别慌）`, `（真的别反着来）`, `（会很亏）`.
- Address words (moderate): `姐妹`, `宝子`, `uu`, `学妹`.
- Punctuation: `～`, `！！`, `？`, `……`.

## Cover/Image Direction

- Use the exact content title as the primary cover headline.
- Vary cover visuals. Do not repeat the same formula.
- For route/list posts: route matrix or school pool board.
- Other options: phone screenshot collage, academic blueprint, editorial
  magazine page, workflow board, minimal typography poster.
- Avoid fake university seals, fake admission letters, or platform logos.

## Keyword Title Angles

- `水博`: myth-busting, route sorting, risk clarification.
- `硕升博`: sequence correction, route comparison, supervisor matching.
- `土博`: employment anxiety, domestic/overseas contrast.
- `升博`: route planning, first-step mistakes, material checklists.

## Compliance Boundaries

- No WeChat IDs, phone numbers, QR codes in body.
- No "加微信/加V/私我领" — use "私信交流/主页有说明".
- No "保过/包上岸/保证录取".
- No fake scarcity ("仅限X名额").
- No extreme words ("最/第一/全网独家").
- All facts must trace to knowledge context or web search results.
